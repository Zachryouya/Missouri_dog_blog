Foxy's life

Foxy now is my ESA dog and she does an amazing job. She loves my husband and she often lay down on the bed whenever she is bored. Foxy and husband love to go walk outside. Whenever, she walks outside, she get scared so she staying by us.


She gives kisses when someone is sad. She also brings her toys or snuggle. She is a hyper dog. Especially, when we have some guests, she will be like a train. She doesn't have any bad intention but we try to train her to be calm down.


We are still working on but she is overall, doing great. Everyone love her. We are hoping that if we have some time, we want to take Foxy to a dog park and let her to meet new friends.
